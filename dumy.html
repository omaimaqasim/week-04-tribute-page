Poet Of East

ALLAMA MUHAMMAD IQBAL

1877 – 1938

"Nations are born in the hearts of poets, they prosper and die in the hands of politicians."

BIOGRAPHY

Early Life

Allama Iqbal was born on 9th November 1877 in Sialkot, Punjab.

He received his early education in Sialkot before moving to Lahore for higher studies.

Iqbal’s birthplace in Sialkot
